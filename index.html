We are a cultural club called Mainland China Student Association. Despite the name, we welcome anyone who is interested in Chinese culture and wants to make friends here. Over the years, we have made ourselves known for reaching out to a wide range of student bodies and various other cultural communities across the Cornell campus while promoting Chinese cultural awareness.
