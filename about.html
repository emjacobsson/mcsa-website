Who We Are

We are Mainland China Student Association. We are committed to promoting Chinese culture on campus and helping students to better navigate their college life and make more friends at Cornell.

We hold three principles for our organization:

We see ourselves as a bridge connecting Chinese students with all other students. We hope to provide an avenue for students to appreciate the beauty of Cornell campus and the people around them, and to fully utilize all the incredible resources available.

We dedicate ourselves to raising Chinese cultural awareness across the Cornell community by organizing various events that aim to introduce interesting Chinese elements to foreign students, faculty, and families.

We want to make Cornell home to everyone. We have a tailored new member development programs to aid in transitioning to Cornell. We also promote our G-body meetings and welcome anyone who is interested to attend our open cultural events.

Big & Little System

We have a special Big and Little System that helps new students navigate their brand-new college life here and foster relationships between students of different ages, interests and backgrounds. Bigs (upperclassmen) provide personal guidance and mentorship to Littles (underclassmen). Underclassmen are paired with upperclassman to form a genuine bond and help them ease into college life.
